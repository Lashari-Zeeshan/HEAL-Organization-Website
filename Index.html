Who We Are

Building a Foundation for Independence

Health Education Action Leadership (HEAL) is dedicated to transforming the lives of the most vulnerable children in Sindh. We believe that every child, regardless of their background, deserves the tools to build a dignified life.

By integrating digital literacy with essential health education and leadership training, we create a holistic ecosystem that fosters independence and civic responsibility.

  • Digital Skills Training
  • Health & Hygiene Awareness
  • Leadership Development

Our Core Purpose

Guided by a clear vision and a strong mission to serve the children of Pakistan.

Our Vision

A society where every orphan and street child in Sindh has the opportunity to thrive, empowered with digital leadership and health skills, and is recognized as a valued and responsible citizen of Pakistan.

Our Mission

HEAL empowers orphanages and street children across Sindh by providing digital, leadership and health skills training, fostering independence, and promoting civic responsibility, shaping a brighter future for themselves and Pakistan.

Our Programs

Holistic development through three key pillars.

Digital Leadership

Equipping children with computer literacy, coding basics, and digital communication skills to compete in the modern world.
